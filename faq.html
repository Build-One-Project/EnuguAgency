Frequently Asked Questions

We have put together the answers to all the most frequently asked questions on our job platform.

WHAT REMITA IS ALL ABOUT?

Remita is a Job seeking platform powered by the Enugu State Government for qualified indivduals who are able to meet up with a Company's criteria.

HOW CAN I APPLY FOR REMITA?

Simply register as an Employer or Job Seeker and create your profile or login to your account.

HOW DOES REMITA FIND JOBS FOR ME?

Employers post jobs on the platform and if you are able to meet up with the given criteria, you send them your Credentials and hope for the best!

CAN I GET A PART-TIME ONLINE JOB FROM REMITA?

This depends on jobs employers post.

In which format and file size can I upload documents?

We prefer a pdf, but you can also upload a word document.

Who can apply for a position at Remita?

At Remita, we embrace diversity. We encourage everyone to apply for our job openings, no matter who you are or what your background is. So if you see an opening that fits with your skills and competencies, don't hold back.
